Cecilia Chiappero

English Language Educator & Researcher | Universidad Nacional de Córdoba

Cecilia Chiappero is an English language educator and researcher affiliated with the Universidad Nacional de Córdoba, Argentina. Her academic work focuses on innovative teaching methodologies and technology-enhanced language learning for vocational insertion.

This A2 English for Hospitality & Services course is the continuation of the A1 course, designed for job insertion in the restaurant, hotel, and cleaning sectors, aligned with the CEFR A2 framework with a strong focus on listening, speaking, and functional communication.

Research Profile: Available on ResearchGate and academic databases.
